CyberNews: 21/12/2025 Edition

Published by Dunateo on 2025-12-21

Today’s roundup

  • RansomHouse upgrades encryption with multi-layered data processing
  • Iranian Infy APT Resurfaces with New Malware Activity After Years of Silence

    • Summary

    The RansomHouse ransomware-as-a-service (RaaS) has recently upgraded its encryptor. The new method replaces a simpler single-phase linear technique with a more complex, multi-layered data processing approach, indicating increased sophistication in their attack capabilities.

    Iranian threat actor Infy, also known as Prince of Persia, has resurfaced with new malware activity after approximately five years of silence. Previously, the group targeted victims in Sweden, the Netherlands, and Turkey. Security researchers from SafeBreach report that the current scale of Infy's operations is more significant than initially anticipated.
